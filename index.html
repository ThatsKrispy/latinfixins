Latin Fixin's – Established in 2012 as a catering company, a year later expanded adding a food truck (our Big Fixins) to be used as a mobile kitchen. Since then we've been adding new trucks to our fleet, partnering with venues and participating in some of south Florida's largest events. You can find us at The Doral Yard open seven days a week

Sunday – Thursday: 11:00 a.m. – 10:00 p.m.
Friday & Saturday: 11:00 a.m. – 11:00 p.m.

We excel in serving delicious foods with impeccable service and can cater any style of event being with or without the foods trucks. We strive in making your event a unique and unforgettable one.
