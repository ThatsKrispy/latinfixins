Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Who We Are

Latin Fixin's ("we," "our," or "us") operates latinfixins.com. We are a food truck and catering company based in Doral, Florida. For questions, contact us at info@latinfixins.com or 786.597.0524.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following when you use our website or contact us:

  • Name, email address, and phone number submitted through contact forms
  • Event details provided in inquiry messages
  • Basic analytics data (pages visited, browser type, device type) via cookies — only if you accept cookies

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to catering and event inquiries
  • To improve our website and user experience
  • To contact you about your booking or event

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to understand how visitors use our site. We only activate non-essential cookies (analytics) after you accept via the cookie consent banner. You may decline cookies at any time — the website remains fully functional without them.

Third-Party Services

Our site may link to or embed services from third parties (Google Maps, UberEats, DoorDash, Instagram, Facebook). These services have their own privacy policies which we encourage you to review. We are not responsible for their data practices.

Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions only as long as needed to respond to your inquiry. You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing info@latinfixins.com.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you. To make a request, contact us at info@latinfixins.com.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
