Our Story

Established 2012. Still Family Run.

Latin Fixin's began as a catering company before expanding with our first food truck, Big Fixins, bringing our bold flavors to events across South Florida. Since then, we've grown our fleet, partnered with premier venues, and served at some of the region's largest events.

We take pride in serving exceptional food with outstanding service and can cater events of any size, with or without our food trucks. Every celebration is thoughtfully crafted to deliver a unique and unforgettable experience.
