The Perfect Peruvian-american fusion

Rethink your traditional American comfort food! Latin Fixin's fusions your old time American staples with everything you love about traditional Peruvian flavors and cooking styles. Featuring unique dishes like the Lomo Saltado burger and the Huancaina Mac & Cheeseburger that will leave you craving for more!

Allow us to provide you with a delicious culinary experience that you will never forget!
